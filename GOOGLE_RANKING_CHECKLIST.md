### 1. Google Search Console (30 min) - MOST IMPORTANT!
**URL:** https://search.google.com/search-console

**Steps:**
1. Click "Add Property"
2. Enter: `https://campusmart.co.ke`
3. Choose verification method:
   - **Option A:** Download HTML file, upload to `/public` folder
   - **Option B:** Copy meta tag, replace `ADD_YOUR_GOOGLE_VERIFICATION_CODE_HERE` in index.html
4. Click "Verify"
5. Submit sitemap: `https://campusmart.co.ke/sitemap.xml`
6. Click "Request Indexing" for homepage

**Why:** Google won't index your site without this!
