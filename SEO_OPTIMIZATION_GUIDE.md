## 🚀 NEXT STEPS TO RANK #1 ON GOOGLE

### **STEP 1: Submit to Google Search Console** (CRITICAL!)

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Add your property: `https://campusmart.co.ke`
3. Verify ownership using one of these methods:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest)
   - **Meta tag** (already in your index.html - replace `ADD_YOUR_GOOGLE_VERIFICATION_CODE_HERE`)
   - **Google Analytics**
   - **DNS record**

4. After verification:
   - Submit your sitemap: `https://campusmart.co.ke/sitemap.xml`
   - Request indexing for your homepage
   - Monitor crawl errors

### **STEP 2: Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools**

1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Add your site
3. Submit sitemap: `https://campusmart.co.ke/sitemap.xml`

### **STEP 3: Create Google My Business Profile**

1. Go to: https://business.google.com
2. Create business profile for "CampusMart Kenya"
3. Add:
   - Business category: "Marketplace" or "Student Service"
   - Location: Nairobi, Kenya
   - Phone number
   - Website: https://campusmart.co.ke
   - Business hours: 24/7 (online)
   - Photos of your platform
