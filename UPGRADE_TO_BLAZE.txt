═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HostPinnacle:
  • Fixed cost: $10-30/month (regardless of usage)
  • Limited resources
  • Manual scaling

Firebase Blaze:
  • Variable cost: $0-20/month (based on actual usage)
  • Unlimited resources
  • Auto-scaling
  • Better performance

For most cases, Firebase is CHEAPER and BETTER!
