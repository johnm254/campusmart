Comrade Safe-Trade Initiative

Trading in Juja and off-campus markets requires extra vigilance. We've built these guidelines to ensure every comrade stays protected.

The Juja Stage Rule

Meet at very busy spots like Juja Stage, Total Petrol Station, or near the Police Station. Never follow a seller into dark alleys or obscure hostels.

Comrade Power

A deal off-campus shouldn't be a solo mission. Carry a fellow comrade along—it's safer and makes negotiation easier when you have backup.

Test-before-PIN

For gadgets, check every function before you think of opening M-Pesa. Once that PIN is entered, the deal is final. Verify everything on the spot.
